Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Zelthir organizes public news coverage and lets signed-in readers save account preferences, interests, personas, and locations.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive the basic account information Google shares with us, such as your email address and profile name. We also store the profile preferences you choose in Zelthir.

How We Use Information

We use your information to keep you signed in, load your saved preferences, personalize story ranking, and operate the news experience.

What We Do Not Sell

We do not sell personal information. We do not use your Google account data for advertising.

Data Providers

Zelthir may send public story text and metadata to service providers that help generate story analysis. Account credentials and session secrets are not included in story analysis requests.
